Vývojka is a Slovak product and brand design studio founded in 2012.

The studio offers wide-ranging services, from furniture design to jewelry and watches, water heaters, bikes and corporate design.

Currently, studio designs wooden, full suspension bikes for children for the company Re pello, Czech Republic.
