For the Re pello brand, we designed a logo, slogan, packaging, Model J manual, flyers, business cards and exhibitions.

We chose font Doko from type designer Ondrej Jób, twelve colors and fourteen color combinations.

We drew illustrations, designed and developed an online store.

The development of corporate design took us three years.

The corporate design of the Re pello brand was created by cooperation between Eva Mikulášová, Júlia Švajda, and Pavol Mikuláš.
